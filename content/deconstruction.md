# deconstruction

Deconstruction is a process of breaking down and examining [[negative feeling thoughts]] to transform them into positive ones. It involves analyzing [[self-talk]] and [[beliefs]] that don't feel good, then systematically reconstructing them into thoughts that generate better feelings. This technique is used to address issues like unworthiness, [[limiting beliefs]], and negative [[thought patterns]], serving as a key method for shifting from low to high [[vibration]] thinking.
