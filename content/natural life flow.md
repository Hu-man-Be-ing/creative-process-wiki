# natural life flow

The effortless [[manifestation]] and experience of desired outcomes when one is in [[alignment]] with high [[vibration]] [[energy]] and free from [[limiting beliefs]]. This represents the unimpeded [[flow]] of [[abundance]], [[well-being]], and positive experiences that occurs when [[resistance]] from [[faulty beliefs]] is removed. [[natural life]] [[flow]] is characterized by ease, [[alignment]] with one's [[inner being]], and the organic unfolding of life in [[harmony]] with one's true [[desires]].
