# stream of well-being

A [[natural flow]] of high [[vibration]] [[energy]] that carries all your [[desires]] to you in a satisfying way. It represents the abundant, positive [[energy]] that is constantly available, similar to a flowing river that moves unobstructed. [[faulty beliefs]] and low vibrations can cause [[resistance]] against this stream, while maintaining high [[vibration]] allows you to receive the [[well-being]] you came for.
