# momentum shift

[[momentum]] shift refers to the gradual process of transitioning from old [[thought habits]] and [[faulty beliefs]] to new, positive [[thought patterns]]. This shift takes time (often 30-90 days or longer) and requires consistent [[practice]] of high-[[vibration]] thinking until a tipping point is reached. It represents the changing of [[beliefs]] and [[energy]] patterns that eventually manifests as different experiences in one's reality.
