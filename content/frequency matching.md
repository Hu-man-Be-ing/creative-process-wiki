# frequency matching

[[frequency]] matching is the process by which the [[vibration]] of your thoughts attracts experiences, people, and circumstances that carry the same vibrational [[frequency]]. When your dominant thoughts are high-[[vibration]]—such as [[appreciation]], [[confidence]], or [[joy]]—you attract matching high-[[vibration]] experiences. When your dominant thoughts are low-[[vibration]]—such as fear, worry, or doubt—you attract experiences that match those lower frequencies.
