# negative feeling thoughts

Negative feeling thoughts are low-[[vibration]] thoughts that prevent the [[natural flow]] of desired outcomes in life. They create emotional discomfort and pain, stemming from [[faulty beliefs]] and misalignment between conscious and [[subconscious mind]] with [[source energy]]. These thoughts lower one's energetic [[frequency]] and attract unwanted experiences, making it important to catch and replace them with higher-[[vibration]] thoughts.
