# vibrational alignment

Vibrational [[alignment]] refers to the state where one's thoughts, emotions, and [[energy]] [[frequency]] match what they desire and who they truly are. When in vibrational [[alignment]], positive emotions indicate that thoughts are supporting desired outcomes, while negative emotions signal misalignment. This [[alignment]] is achieved through feel-good thoughts and high-[[vibration]] thinking, which allows the [[law of attraction]] to bring desired experiences into one's reality.
