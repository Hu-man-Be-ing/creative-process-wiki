# natural self

Natural self refers to one's authentic, true identity that exists before conditioning by [[limiting beliefs]] and societal influences. It represents the [[wholeness]] and completeness that people possess from birth, including natural [[self-worth]], [[self-acceptance]], [[self-confidence]], and [[self-love]]. The natural self embodies one's inherent gifts, talents, and high-[[vibration]] state that becomes obscured by [[faulty beliefs]] acquired during childhood and life experiences.
