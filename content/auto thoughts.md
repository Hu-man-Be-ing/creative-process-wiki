# auto thoughts

[[automatic thoughts]] generated by subconscious [[beliefs]] that have been repeated so many times they become default mental broadcasts. These thoughts pop into your mind readily based on stored [[beliefs]] and past programming, often from faulty or [[limiting beliefs]]. While auto thoughts can be overridden consciously, the goal is to clean up subconscious [[beliefs]] so that auto thoughts naturally support positive outcomes and [[alignment]] with what you want.
