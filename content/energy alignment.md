# energy alignment

[[energy]] [[alignment]] occurs when one's [[present moment]] thoughts and conscious [[awareness]] are in [[harmony]] with their [[source energy]] [[frequency]]. It represents a state where an individual's vibrational [[energy]] matches their natural high-[[vibration]] state, [[allowing]] life to [[flow]] more easily. Achieving [[energy]] [[alignment]] is considered the solution to life's challenges and enables natural [[manifestation]] of desired experiences.
