# confidence

Confidence represents [[trust]] and [[faith]] in one's abilities, [[worthiness]], and capacity to create desired life experiences. It grows through consistent [[practice]] of positive thoughts and [[alignment]] with one's [[source self]], developing into [[expectation]] and [[excitement]] about [[manifesting]] goals. Confidence is closely connected to [[self-respect]], [[self-love]], and [[self-acceptance]], and serves as essential foundation for taking charge of one's life and believing in the possibility of having good experiences.
