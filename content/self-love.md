# self-love

Self-[[love]] is the natural, unconditional [[acceptance]] and [[appreciation]] of oneself, forming a cornerstone of personal [[well-being]] and high [[vibration]] living. It encompasses [[self-worth]], [[self-image]], [[self-acceptance]], and self-esteem, though it often becomes damaged through life experiences from childhood into adulthood. Cultivating self-[[love]] is essential for maintaining a positive relationship with oneself and attracting aligned experiences and [[relationships]].
