# natural life

Natural life refers to a state of [[joyous expansion]] and [[unlimited abundance]] that flows effortlessly when one lives authentically without interference from [[faulty beliefs]] or external standards. It is characterized by the continuous [[flow]] of desired experiences and represents the pure, high-[[vibration]] existence that individuals came here to experience. This contrasts with life lived according to others' expectations or societal standards that have nothing to do with one's [[authentic self]].
