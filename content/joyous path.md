# joyous path

The joyous [[path]] refers to the easy and effortless route to receiving one's [[desires]] and living the life one came for. It is accessed through maintaining [[good feeling thoughts]] and high [[vibration]] [[energy]], which allows the [[law of attraction]] to connect you to positive manifestations. Life is meant to be joyous, and this [[path]] represents the [[natural flow]] of [[abundance]] and wanted experiences.
