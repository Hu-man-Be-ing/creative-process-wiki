# thought training

Thought training is a mental [[practice]] focused on replacing negative or low-[[vibration]] thoughts with positive, good-feeling thoughts to improve one's overall [[vibration]] and [[well-being]]. It involves using specific tools, techniques, and methods to consciously choose better thoughts throughout the day. This [[practice]] is described as being like going to a "thought training gym" where individuals work to retrain their [[consciousness]] and establish new mental habits.
