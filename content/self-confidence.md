# self-confidence

Self-[[confidence]] is described as a fundamental component of one's sense of self, alongside [[self-worth]], [[self-acceptance]], and [[self-image]]. It represents the belief in oneself and one's abilities, which is essential for projecting positive [[energy]] and achieving desired outcomes in life. The context suggests that self-[[confidence]] is natural to humans but can be damaged by life experiences, and restoring it is crucial for living authentically.
