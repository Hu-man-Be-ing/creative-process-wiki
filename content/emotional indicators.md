# emotional indicators

Emotional indicators are the feelings you experience that reveal the [[vibration]] of the thoughts you are currently thinking. Positive emotions—such as [[joy]], [[appreciation]], [[confidence]], or [[enthusiasm]]—indicate that your thoughts are aligned with your [[desires]] and are high [[vibration]]. Negative emotions—such as fear, worry, frustration, or guilt—indicate that your thoughts are [[out of alignment]] and are offering a low [[vibration]].
