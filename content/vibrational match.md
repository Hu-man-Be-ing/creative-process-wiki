# vibrational match

A vibrational match occurs when one's dominant present-moment [[vibration]] aligns with the [[frequency]] of what they desire to manifest. When someone becomes a vibrational match through high [[vibration]] thoughts and feelings, the [[law of attraction]] can connect them with their [[desires]] and the [[path]] to achieving them. Being out of [[vibrational alignment]] with [[desires]] prevents [[manifestation]] and attracts experiences that match the actual [[vibration]] being offered.
