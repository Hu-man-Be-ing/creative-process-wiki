# seven stages of manifestation change

A methodical framework that tracks the progressive changes that occur when learning and applying knowledge about [[manifestation]] and [[vibration]]. These stages help individuals understand where they are in the process of cleaning up their [[energy]] and moving from low [[vibration]] to high [[vibration]] living. The stages show the lag time between making vibrational shifts and seeing actual [[manifestation]] results, providing guidance through the [[transformation]] process.

7 stages:
1) Education - knowledge of life / positive mind focus

2) Accept / Trust the process  (trust positive focus)

3) Start living / tracking progress

4) Notice feeling increasing calm / ease  (more positive emotion / less negative)
   Trusting the process

5) Notice days flowing with more ease, and things that look like your desire
   Trusting the process, inspired action

6) Notice your physical life manifestations trending towards what you want

7) Notice your physical life manifesting in the desired ways
