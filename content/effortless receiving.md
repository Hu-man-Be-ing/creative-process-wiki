# effortless receiving

Effortless receiving refers to the natural, joyous way of attracting and [[manifesting]] desired experiences without struggle or force. It occurs when one maintains high [[vibrational alignment]] and [[positive focus]], [[allowing]] the [[law of attraction]] to deliver what is wanted through [[inspired action]] and divine timing. This state represents life's [[natural flow]] of [[expansion]] and [[abundance]], free from hardship or [[resistance]].
