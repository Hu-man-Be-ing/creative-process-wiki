# law of attraction

The law of attraction is a universal principle that states [[energy]] frequencies (vibrations) attract similar energies, connecting us with experiences that match our dominant [[vibration]]. It responds to the [[energy]] we constantly emit through our [[beliefs]] and thoughts, bringing us together with people, circumstances, and experiences that vibrationally match what we're broadcasting. The law orchestrates all the details to connect us with what we want when our [[vibration]] aligns with our [[desires]].
