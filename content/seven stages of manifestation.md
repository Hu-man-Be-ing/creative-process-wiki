# seven stages of manifestation

The seven stages of [[manifestation]] is a framework that outlines the progression from desire to physical [[manifestation]]. The stages include movement from lower vibrational states (stages 1-3) to higher states where [[flow]] begins (stage 4), continues (stage 5), [[trending manifestations]] appear (stage 6), and full [[manifestation]] occurs (stage 7). This process emphasizes that [[manifestation]] is about progressing through vibrational stages rather than using specific techniques.
