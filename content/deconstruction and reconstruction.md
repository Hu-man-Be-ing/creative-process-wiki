# deconstruction and reconstruction

[[deconstruction]] and [[reconstruction]] is a mental process for handling out-of-[[alignment]] thoughts by first acknowledging and breaking them down ([[deconstruction]]), then rebuilding them into more positive, aligned thinking ([[reconstruction]]). This technique helps individuals get unstuck from negative [[thought patterns]] and reprogram their [[subconscious mind]]. The process is designed to quickly shift someone from a negative emotional state back into a positive feeling place.
