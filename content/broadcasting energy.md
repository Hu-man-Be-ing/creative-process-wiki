# broadcasting energy

Broadcasting [[energy]] describes how individuals constantly project or transmit vibrational [[energy]] through their thoughts and subconscious [[beliefs]], similar to how a projector displays images. This [[energy]] broadcast operates at all times and determines what manifests in one's life through the [[law of attraction]], which responds to the quality of [[energy]] being transmitted. The goal is to intentionally broadcast high [[vibration]] [[energy]] rather than [[allowing]] default, potentially negative [[energy]] patterns to operate unconsciously.
