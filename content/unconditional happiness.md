# unconditional happiness

Unconditional happiness is a state of [[feeling good]] and joyful without needing external conditions or circumstances to be met. It represents the natural high-vibrational state of being that humans are born with, characterized by [[joy]], [[peace]], and [[satisfaction]] that exists independently of what manifests in [[physical reality]]. This happiness comes from understanding how life works and knowing that one's [[inner being]] is inherently positive.
