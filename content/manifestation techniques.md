# manifestation techniques

[[manifestation]] techniques refer to various methods people use to try to attract or create desired outcomes in their lives, such as [[meditation]], vision boards, and affirmations. However, according to the context, these techniques are considered ineffective because there are no shortcuts to [[manifestation]]. True [[manifestation]] comes from maintaining high [[vibration]] [[energy]] and retraining daily [[thought patterns]] rather than relying on specific techniques.
