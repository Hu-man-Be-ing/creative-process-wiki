# vibrational reality

Vibrational reality is the immediate [[manifestation]] of [[desires]] and [[dreams]] in energetic form that occurs instantly when we ask for what we want. It exists as a non-physical realm where our thoughts, [[desires]], and [[dreams]] are created by our [[inner being]] before they manifest into [[physical reality]]. This vibrational reality serves as the bridge between our thoughts and the physical [[manifestation]] of our [[desires]].
